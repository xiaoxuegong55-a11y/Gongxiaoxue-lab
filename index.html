About

Founder / Researcher / Builder

I am Xiaoxue Gong, with training in public health and preventive medicine and hands-on experience in pulmonary fibrosis, primary cell platforms, preclinical models, multi-omics analysis, and mechanism-oriented research. This site is not just a resume extension. It is designed as a personal lab-style brand to organize ideas, communicate research direction, and build a long-term virtual infrastructure for fibrosis-focused scientific work.

  • Research themes: pulmonary fibrosis, tissue repair, stem-cell-associated mechanisms, translational target exploration
  • Technical base: primary cell isolation and culture, mouse fibrosis models, scRNA-seq, spatial transcriptomics, proteomics
